How to Use Dance Motion Tracker
Step 1: Upload Video

Upload a dance video in MP4, AVI, MOV, or other supported formats. Best results with clear, well-lit videos.

Step 2: Configure Processing

Choose your processing options:

  • Apply smoothing to reduce jitter
  • Select export formats (JSON, CSV, Blender)
  • Enable overlay video creation
Step 3: Process

Click "Start Processing" to analyze the video and extract pose data using MediaPipe.

Step 4: Download Results

Download the processed data:

  • Pose data (JSON/CSV)
  • Blender-compatible files
  • Video with pose overlay
  • Analysis statistics
Tips for Best Results
  • Use well-lit videos with clear visibility of the dancer
  • Avoid cluttered backgrounds when possible
  • Higher resolution videos (1080p) provide better accuracy
  • Ensure the full body is visible in most frames
